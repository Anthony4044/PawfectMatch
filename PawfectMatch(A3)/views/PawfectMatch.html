Welcome to Pawfect Match!


We are a non-profit organization dedicated to finding loving homes for abandoned and unwanted cats and dogs. All the animals in our care have been vet-checked and are up-to-date on their vaccinations. We encourage you to browse through our available pets and consider giving one a forever home. You can also find resources for caring for your new furry friend on our Dog Care and Cat Care pages.
